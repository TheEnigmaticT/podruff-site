# Copywriting Rules

## Core Principle

Extract and repurpose the speaker's actual words. Never invent content. The client's voice is the star — you are amplifying it, not replacing it.

## Frameworks

### PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
- **Problem:** Call out the pain the audience feels
- **Agitation:** Twist the knife — make the cost of inaction vivid
- **Solution:** Present the relief using the speaker's own insight

### Value Props (So What?)
After every claim, ask "so what?" until you hit emotion.

"We use AI" -> So what?
"To automate your workflows" -> So what?
"So you save 10 hours/week" -> So what?
"So you can focus on what matters" -> That's the copy.

### CTAs (Action + Benefit)
Not: "Learn More" / "Click Here" / "Check it out"
But: "Get the framework" / "Try this today" / "Steal this approach"

## Style Rules

- Lead with the most surprising or counterintuitive claim
- Short sentences. Fragments for emphasis. One idea per sentence.
- First person or second person. Never third person corporate voice.
- Use contractions. "Don't" not "do not."
- Specific numbers over vague claims. "3x pipeline" not "significant growth."
- End with a question, a challenge, or a one-line takeaway — not a summary.

## Cut Ruthlessly

Delete:
- "that" (usually unnecessary)
- "very" and "really"
- "in order to" (use "to")
- Throat-clearing ("I think", "It's worth noting", "Interestingly")
- Any sentence that restates what you just said

## BANNED WORDS

Never use these. They are AI-tells that make copy sound generated:

Annals, Employ, Bustling, Indeed, Enigma, Indelible, Meticulous, Esteemed, Whimsical, Bespoke, Captivate, Paradigm, Underscore, Embrace, Nuance, Dynamic, Ethos, Elevate, Leverage, Resonate, Foster, Endeavor, Embark, Unleash, Innovative, Solution, Optimize, Streamline, Cutting-edge, Revolutionary, Empower, Synergy, Holistic, Curate, Game-changer, Transform, Revolutionize, Future-proof, Strategic, Supercharge, Harness, Fundamental, Enhanced, Unprecedented, Seamless, Powerful, Intuitive, Comprehensive, Tailored, Scalable, Agile, Best-in-class, Next-generation, Enable, Crucial, Essential, Robust, Proactive, Delve, Emphasize, Aligned, Moreover, Thrilled

## BANNED PHRASES

These appear 50-400x more frequently in AI writing than human writing:

- "provide a valuable/comprehensive insight"
- "left an indelible mark"
- "play a significant/crucial/vital role in"
- "unwavering commitment/support"
- "stark reminder"
- "gain a deeper/nuanced understanding"
- "comprehensive overview"
- "multifaceted nature/approach"
- "shed light on"
- "navigate the complex/challenge"
- "potential to revolutionize"
- "relentless pursuit"
- "transformative power"
- "today the fast pace of the world"
- "significant milestone/turning point"
- "delve deeper into"
- "unique blend"
- "commitment to excellence"
- "pave the way for the future"
- "vital role in shaping"
- "In this article/guide/blog post..."
- "It is important to note that..."
- "Master the art of..."
- "In summary/conclusion..."
- "A testament to..."
- "In the dynamic world of..."
- "Embark on a journey..."
