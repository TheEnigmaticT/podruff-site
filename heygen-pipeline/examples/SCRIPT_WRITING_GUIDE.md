# Quick Tips for Writing Scripts

## Length Guidelines

| Duration | Word Count | Characters |
|----------|------------|------------|
| 15 seconds | ~40 words | ~250 chars |
| 30 seconds | ~75 words | ~500 chars |
| 60 seconds | ~150 words | ~1000 chars |

## Writing for AI Avatars

### ✅ Do:
- Write conversational, natural language
- Use contractions (don't, can't, it's)
- Add commas for natural pauses
- Read it out loud to check flow
- Use active voice

### ❌ Don't:
- Use complex jargon
- Write overly long sentences
- Include special characters or emojis
- Use abbreviations without context
- Write in bullet points

## Script Structure

### Hook (First 5 seconds)
Grab attention immediately:
- "Did you know that..."
- "Stop making this mistake..."
- "What if I told you..."

### Body (Main Content)
Deliver your message:
- Keep paragraphs short
- One idea per sentence
- Use transition words

### CTA (Last 5 seconds)
Tell them what to do:
- "Click the link below"
- "Visit our website"
- "Book a call today"

## Example Scripts

### Product Demo (60s)
```
Are you struggling to [common problem]? You're not alone. Thousands of businesses face this challenge every day. But what if there was a better way? Introducing [Product Name]. Our solution helps you [benefit 1], [benefit 2], and [benefit 3] - all in one simple platform. Here's how it works. First, you [step 1]. Then, [step 2]. Finally, [step 3]. It's that simple. Companies like [example] have seen [result] in just [timeframe]. Ready to see the difference? Try [Product Name] free for 14 days. Click the link and get started today.
```

### Social Media Hook (15s)
```
Stop doing [common mistake] right now! Here's what actually works. [Share quick tip]. Follow for more!
```

### Testimonial (45s)
```
Hi, I'm [name] from [company]. We were struggling with [problem] for months. Then we found [solution]. In just [timeframe], everything changed. We saw [specific result]. The best part? [unexpected benefit]. I only wish we'd started sooner. If you're on the fence, just do it. You won't regret it.
```
