You know that feeling. Thirty minutes into a conversation, your guest says something that would stop a scroller mid-thumb. A perfect hook. You think: I'll remember that.

You won't. By the time you hit stop, it's gone. Buried under forty minutes of audio you don't have time to re-listen to. You end up scrubbing through a waveform at 2am, looking for eight seconds you half-remember.

That's not a workflow. That's a lottery. Podruff sits in the background and catches those moments in real time. You don't need to pause, scribble, or break eye contact with your guest.

How it works

1

Route your audio

Point Podruff at your call's audio output. Works with Zoom, Riverside, SquadCast — anything with a mic signal.

2

Hit record

Start your conversation. Podruff transcribes in the background and flags potential hooks as they happen. No tab-switching required.

3

Grab the good ones

When you're done, your hooks are waiting. Timestamped, quoted, ready to drop into your show notes or social posts.

Hooks while you're still talking

Most tools make you wait until after the recording. By then, you've lost context. Podruff identifies quotable moments during the conversation, so the list is ready the second you hang up. No scrubbing. No re-listening. No guessing which minute had the gold.

Your data stays on your machine

Audio never leaves your computer. Transcription and hook detection run locally. No cloud uploads, no third-party processing, no wondering who else is listening to your pre-release interview. Your recordings are yours.
